# Marketing Onboarding Guide

## First 30 Days
- Complete onboarding training modules T1–T4
- Review the Acme Corp Brand Guidelines (Confluence: MKT-BRAND)
- Get access to HubSpot, Google Analytics, Canva, and Semrush (request via IT portal)
- Meet with your pod lead to understand current campaign priorities
- Review the last 3 monthly marketing reports in Confluence: MKT-REPORTS

## Brand Guidelines Summary
- **Logo**: Always use approved SVG files from Brandfolder; do not recreate or alter the logo
- **Colors**: Primary — Acme Blue (#1E40AF), Secondary — Acme Teal (#0D9488); use brand palette only
- **Typography**: Inter (web), Aktiv Grotesk (print); no other fonts in external materials
- **Tone of voice**: Confident, clear, human — avoid jargon; speak to practitioners, not executives
- All external materials must be reviewed by the Brand team before publication
- Full guidelines: Brandfolder > Acme Corp > Brand Guidelines 2026

## Campaign Process
1. **Brief** — Submit a campaign brief using the template in Confluence: MKT-BRIEF
2. **Approval** — Manager approves brief; Brand reviews creative assets
3. **Build** — Use HubSpot for email campaigns, Marketo for nurture sequences (L3+)
4. **Launch** — Sign-off from manager required before going live; schedule via content calendar
5. **Report** — Post-campaign report due within 5 business days of campaign end

## Content Calendar
The shared content calendar lives in Notion (MKT-CALENDAR). All campaigns, blog posts, social posts, and events must be added at least 2 weeks in advance. Content calendar is reviewed in the Monday marketing all-hands.

## Analytics and Reporting
- Primary analytics: Google Analytics 4 and HubSpot
- Monthly reports are due on the 5th of the following month
- KPIs tracked: MQLs, pipeline influenced, website traffic, CAC, content engagement
- Tableau dashboard for executive reporting (L4+ access)
- Use UTM parameters on all external links — UTM builder in Confluence: MKT-UTM

## Demand Generation
- Paid campaigns managed in Google Ads and LinkedIn Campaign Manager (L4+ only for budget control)
- SEO strategy documented in Semrush; keyword research must be approved by SEO lead
- Events (webinars, conferences) require 6-week lead time; event brief template in Confluence

## Key Contacts

- **Web team**: web@acme.com — Landing page requests, website changes
- **Sales alignment**: sales-marketing@acme.com — MQL handoff, SLA questions
