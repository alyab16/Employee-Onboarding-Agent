# Acme Corp Benefits Guide

## Health Insurance
Acme Corp covers 90% of premiums for employees and 75% for dependents.

Plans available:
- **Acme Plus (PPO)**: Low deductible ($500 individual), broad network, no referrals needed
- **Acme Select (HDHP)**: High deductible ($1,500 individual) paired with a Health Savings Account (HSA); company contributes $500/year to your HSA
- **Kaiser HMO** (California only): $0 deductible, must use Kaiser network

Dental and vision are included at no additional premium cost. Dental covers two cleanings per year and up to $2,000 in restorative work annually.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan
- Company match: 100% of your contributions up to 4% of salary
- Vesting: immediate — the match is yours from day one
- Plan provider: Fidelity
- Contribution changes take effect the first of the following month
- Enroll or change contributions at netbenefits.fidelity.com

## Equity / RSUs
- L1–L2: 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff, quarterly thereafter
- L3–L4: Refreshes granted at each performance review based on rating
- L5+: Annual refresh grants; compensation committee approval required for grants over $200k
- RSUs are taxed as ordinary income upon vesting

## Wellness Stipend
All employees receive $500 per calendar year reimbursable for:
- Gym memberships, fitness classes, home exercise equipment
- Mental health apps (Calm, Headspace, Noom)
- Ergonomic home office equipment
Submit receipts via Expensify with category "Wellness Stipend."

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
Free and confidential counseling sessions (up to 8 per year), financial coaching, and legal consultations through LifeWorks. Access via 1-800-ACME-EAP or lifeworks.com/acme.

## Other Perks
- **Commuter benefits**: Pre-tax transit/parking up to $315/month
- **Home office stipend**: $500 one-time for new hires to set up a home workspace
- **Company events**: Quarterly all-hands, annual company retreat
- **Referral bonus**: $3,000 for successful full-time referrals (paid 90 days after start date)
- **Volunteer time off**: 2 paid days per year for community service

## Open Enrollment
Open enrollment runs in November each year for January 1 effective dates. Changes outside of open enrollment require a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, loss of other coverage).
