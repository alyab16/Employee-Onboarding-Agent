# Acme Corp HR Policies

## Time Off (PTO)
- L1–L2: 20 days paid time off per year
- L3–L4: 25 days paid time off per year
- L5+: Flexible/unlimited PTO with manager approval
- PTO must be requested at least 3 business days in advance for planned absences
- Unused PTO up to 10 days rolls over to the next calendar year

## Sick Leave
All employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, separate from PTO. No carryover.

## Remote Work Policy
- L1–L2: Minimum 3 days in office per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday)
- L3–L4: Minimum 2 days in office per week
- L5+: Flexible; coordinate with your manager
- Remote work from outside the country requires approval from HR and legal at least 4 weeks in advance

## Parental Leave
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid
- Secondary caregiver: 6 weeks fully paid
- Applies to birth, adoption, and foster placements
- Notify HR at least 30 days before the expected leave start date

## Performance Reviews
- Conducted bi-annually: June and December
- Employees set OKRs at the start of each cycle
- 360-degree feedback collected from peers, direct reports, and manager
- Compensation adjustments linked to performance review outcomes
- Promotion eligibility reviewed in December cycle

## Expense Reimbursement
- Meals: up to $75 per person for client entertainment, $30 for team meals
- Travel: economy class for flights under 4 hours; business class eligible for L4+
- Submit expenses within 30 days of purchase via Expensify
- All expenses over $500 require pre-approval from your manager

## Learning & Development Budget
- L1–L3: $1,000 per calendar year for courses, conferences, books
- L4–L5: $2,000 per calendar year
- Prior manager approval required for expenses over $200
- Unused budget does not roll over

## Conflict Resolution
Employees with workplace concerns should first speak directly with the involved party. If unresolved, escalate to your manager, then to HR. HR guarantees confidentiality and a response within 5 business days.
