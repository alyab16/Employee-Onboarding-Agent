# Sales Onboarding Guide

## First 30 Days
- Complete onboarding training modules T1–T4
- Attend Sales Bootcamp (first Monday of each month, full day)
- Shadow 3 discovery calls and 2 demo calls with senior AEs
- Complete Salesforce CRM certification (online, ~4 hours) — link in Confluence: SALES-CRM
- Set up Gong, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, and Clari by end of week 1

## Sales Process
Acme Corp uses a 6-stage sales process (tracked in Salesforce):
1. **Prospecting** — Identify and qualify leads using LinkedIn Sales Navigator + ZoomInfo
2. **Discovery** — Understand pain points; use MEDDIC qualification framework
3. **Demo** — Tailored product demonstration; request SE support for technical demos
4. **Proposal** — Generate SOW via Salesforce CPQ (L3+); standard contracts via DocuSign
5. **Negotiation** — Discounts >15% require VP approval; document all concessions in Salesforce
6. **Closed/Won** — Handoff to Customer Success via the CS handoff template in Confluence

## CRM Hygiene (Required)
- All customer interactions must be logged in Salesforce within 24 hours
- Opportunity stages and close dates must be accurate at all times
- Gong automatically records and syncs all calls — review your calls within 48 hours
- Weekly pipeline review with your manager: Clari used for forecasting

## Quota and Compensation
- Quota is set quarterly based on territory, segment, and company targets
- At-plan: 100% quota attainment = on-target earnings (OTE)
- Accelerators kick in above 100%: 110% for 100–110% attainment, 120% for 110%+
- New hires have a 90-day ramp: 50% quota month 1, 75% month 2, 100% month 3

## Territory Assignment
Your territory is defined by geography, industry vertical, or named accounts (varies by role and level). Territory details are in your offer letter and Salesforce.
- Do not contact accounts outside your territory without approval from your manager
- Territory disputes must be escalated to Revenue Operations (revops@acme.com)

## Key Contacts
- **Revenue Operations**: revops@acme.com — Salesforce issues, territory, comp questions
- **Sales Engineering**: se-team@acme.com — Request SE support for technical demos
- **Deal Desk**: dealdesk@acme.com — Non-standard pricing, contract terms
