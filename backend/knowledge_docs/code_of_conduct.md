# Acme Corp Code of Conduct

## Our Core Values
- **Integrity**: We do what we say and say what we mean. We are honest with customers, partners, and each other.
- **Innovation**: We encourage bold ideas and are not afraid to fail fast and learn.
- **Inclusion**: Every voice matters. We build an environment where diverse perspectives are welcomed.
- **Customer Obsession**: Our customers' success defines our success.

## Expected Behavior
All employees must:
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity
- Maintain confidentiality of company and customer information
- Represent Acme Corp professionally in all public communications
- Report potential violations promptly through proper channels
- Complete all mandatory compliance training by their deadlines

## Conflicts of Interest
A conflict of interest exists when personal interests could interfere with Acme Corp's interests. Examples include:
- Holding a financial interest in a competitor or supplier
- Employing or contracting a family member in a reporting relationship
- Accepting gifts over $50 in value from vendors or customers
- Working for a competitor or starting a competing business while employed

All potential conflicts must be disclosed to HR in writing within 10 business days of discovery.

## Anti-Harassment Policy
Acme Corp has zero tolerance for harassment of any kind, including:
- Verbal or written harassment based on protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual advances or requests for sexual favors
- Creating a hostile work environment through bullying, intimidation, or exclusion

Any employee who experiences or witnesses harassment should report it immediately to HR or their manager. Retaliation against anyone who makes a good-faith report is strictly prohibited and is itself a terminable offense.

## Use of Company Resources
- Company devices, systems, and accounts are for business use; limited personal use is permitted
- Do not use company resources to engage in political activities, personal businesses, or illegal activity
- All work products created using company resources are owned by Acme Corp

## Social Media
- Do not share confidential company information or unreleased product details on social media
- You may identify yourself as an Acme Corp employee but make clear that views are your own
- Do not make disparaging comments about customers, competitors, or colleagues

## Reporting Violations
Report violations via:
1. Direct conversation with your manager
2. HR at ethics@acme.com
3. Anonymous ethics hotline: 1-800-ACME-ETH

All reports are investigated confidentially. Substantiated violations result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
